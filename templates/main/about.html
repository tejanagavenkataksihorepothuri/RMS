About Usha Rama College of Engineering

Usha Rama College of Engineering and Technology (URCET) was established in 2008 under the aegis of Sai Durga Educational Society. The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to JNTUK, Kakinada. The college offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

Programs Offered
  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
